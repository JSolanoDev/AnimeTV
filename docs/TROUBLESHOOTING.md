# Troubleshooting

## Anime1v says quota reached

Anime1v may return:

```text
Limite de requests alcanzado. Tu plan permite 100 requests/dia.
```

AnimeTV will pause Anime1v for the quota window and keep AniPub/JIMOV active.

## Android TV cannot reach 127.0.0.1

Use your PC LAN IP from the TV, not `127.0.0.1`.

## Video closes but audio continues

Use the in-app close button or close the detail overlay. AnimeTV clears iframe/video sources when closing.
